Intro

I am Ma. Leonardo Ma. Who am I? I am a designer and programmer. I am Hong Kong-born & Vancouver-raised. As a designer, my direction is influenced by the harmonious structure of Chinese characters, considering every detail of my design and how they can successfully fit together. As a programmer, I am constantly hungry for knowledge. I love getting my hands dirty when tackling challenges.
When I'm in the zone, my approach embodies three qualities of design and coding: meticulousness, omnipresence, and sustainability. By combining the meticulousness in problem solving and making conscious decisions, I strive to maintain sustainable designs and code.
